The Engineer Hackathon NITK's Annual Technical Fest Hackathon

March 4th-5th 2022

The Engineer Hackathon

The Engineer Hackathon, which is a part of NITK's Annual Technical Fest, is a 24-hour long hackathon that gives you an opportunity to build something unique to solve problems from various spheres of modern human life. Use this hackathon to launch your big idea and potentially get continuity grants to turn your idea into a real-world product!

Themes

Blockchain

Intersted in Ethereum development?
Have a cool idea to use blockchain to solve a problem?
This is your time to shine! Use a Blockchain to build a project that is intersting as well as useful, and stand a chance to win cash prizes, goodies and a continuity grant to turn your project into a real-world product!

AI/ML

Do Neural Networks interst you?
Do you have an intersting use case for Machine Learning solutions?
Build a project involving Machine Learning techniques or models and stand a chance to prizes!

HealthTech

Interested in making Health Care more accessible?
Want to make Health Care systems more streamlined?
Can you think of ways to use new technologies to improve Health Care?
Think of a problem in Health Care that you feel deserves more attention, work on a solution to solve the problem, and stand a chance to win cash prizes and goodies!

Have an intersting idea that isn't related to the themes above? We'd love to hear about it nonetheless!

Although these are some suggested themes, you don't have to limit yourself to these.
Join the Hackathon Discord server for more information!

Prizes

Polygon (previously Matic) will be giving away a cash prize of 10,000 INR for the best hack built on top of Ethereum during the hackathon.

For teams that integrate Polygon in their hacks as well, the prize money will be higher, 15,000 INR along with eligibility for internship/full-time role interviews and a chance to land seed funding of upto 5000 USD!

The Tezos Foundation will be giving away:
  • A cash prize of 20000 INR for the best DApp built on Tezos
  • Every valid submission made in the Tezos track will be rewarded with exclusive Schwag Kits
  • Continuity grant opportunity up to 5,000 USD for an outstanding project

The Celo Foundation is giving away 20000 INR for the best Dapp built on Celo

Protocol Labs is giving away 20000 INR to a project which makes the best use of IPFS and/or Filecoin

GeeksforGeeks goodies and vouchers worth 12000 INR to each of the top 4 teams!

Lumos Labs will be giving away 7000 INR and 5000 INR to the first and second winner respectively under the Cudos India Hackathon Track! (To be considered for this track, you need to use the Cudos platform as a part of your project)

FAQs


  • What is the mode of the Hackathon, online or offline?

    The Hackathon will be conducted in online mode. However, we also have a dedicated venue on campus, where participants can get together and work offline

  • Can I attend the Hackathon offline?

    Yes, you can come to NITK and attend the Hackathon. Please note that if you are not a student of NITK, you will need to purchase the Engineer 2022 Entry Pass in order to enter the campus during the Fest. The passes can be bought from the events page.

  • What is eligibility criteria for the Hackathon?

    The hackathon is open to all! :)

  • What is the duration of the Hackathon?

    This a 24-hour long hackathon

  • How many people can be on a team?

    Teams can have up to 4 people

  • Will accomodation be provided to out-of-town participants?

    All offline participants can use the dedicated offline venue to participate in the hackathon. But we will not be able to provide separate accomodation to out-of-town participants. However, such participants can avail accomodation at the NITK Guest House

  • Is there any registration fee for the Hackathon?

    No. The Hackathon is free for all participants

  • What counts as a valid submission to the Tezos track?

    Refer to this notion page for more details on submission guidelines.
